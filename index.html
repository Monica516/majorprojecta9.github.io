ABOUT STUTTERING

Stuttering

Stuttering is characterized by repetition of sounds, syllables, or words, as well as prolongation of sounds and blocks, where the person is unable to produce any sound at all.

Statistics

An estimated 11 to 12 million people in India stammer according to the World Health Organization. Stuttering is seen in about 5% of children and about 1% of the adult population.

Problems

Stuttering may cause developmental problems such as Communication difficulties, Social Isolation, Emotional distress, Academic challenges and also Career limitations

Early Diagnosis

Early identification of stuttering in children is crucial for successful intervention and can have a significant impact on their development such as children can have earlier access to speech therapy.
